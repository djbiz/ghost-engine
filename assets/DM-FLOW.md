# Ghost Monetization Engine — DM Flow 2.0
## Lethal Close-Level Scripts

---

## COLD OUTREACH

### Opener (Pick One)

**Option A — Pattern Interrupt**
"Just stalked your TikTok — solid traffic, engaged audience, but I'm seeing zero monetization setup. You thinking about that yet?"

**Option B — Direct Observation**
"Quick question — you post consistently, get decent engagement, but I'm guessing the bank account doesn't match the views. Am I off?"

**Option C — Authority Angle**
"Yo — I help creators like you turn attention into revenue systems. Not content, not coaching. Actual money. You currently set up with any offers?"

---

## AFTER FIRST REPLY

**If they say yes (they have something):**
"Nice — what are you running? And what's the gap — traffic, offer, or conversion?"

**If they say no (nothing yet):**
"That's actually the easiest fix. Once you have attention and no system, the first move is always the same. You looking to fix that?"

---

## MID-CONVERSATION BRIDGE

After they've engaged (3–5 messages in):

"So what stopped you from monetizing already? Was it the offer, the tech, or just not knowing where to start?"

Listen to their answer → match to tier.
