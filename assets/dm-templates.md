## TIER 1: Quick Flip (5K–20K followers)

### Opener
```
Hey [name] — quick question.

Are you guys intentionally leaving money on the table with your audience, or is that just how it's been shaking out?

I ask because I've been tracking creator monetization gaps — and most people posting to 10K-20K are one simple offer away from their first $1K month.

Curious if that's already handled on your end.
```

### If No Response (Day 4)
```
Still thinking about this one.

Not trying to sell you anything — just figured it was worth a flag.

Most creators I talk to don't realize how close they are until we actually map it out.

No pressure either way. But if you're curious, happy to show you what I'm seeing.
```

### If Engaged — Value Drop
```
Here's what I'll tell you for free:

The gap isn't usually content. It's offer structure.

You already have attention. The question is whether you have something to sell them that actually converts.

Most people don't — they just wing it with a Linktree and hope.

If you want, I can map out what a simple $49-$99 offer could look like for your audience. Takes 20 minutes, might be worth a lot more.

Worst case: you get a clean framework. Best case: you launch something that actually makes money.
```

### Close
```
Look — I'm not here to waste your time.

If this is already handled, just say the word and I'll leave you alone.

If it's not — let's hop on a 15-minute call and I'll show you exactly what I'd build for you.

I only take on a few of these a month anyway. Could be a good fit.
```
