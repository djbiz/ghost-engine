## PHASE 1 — "CLOSE HEAVY" — Days 1–10

**Goal: $10K–$20K fast. Build momentum. Get proof.**

### 🔥 Target ONLY These Leads

Stop spraying. Filter for:
- **20K–150K followers**
- **Consistent posting** (posted in last 48h)
- **Clear engagement** (comments, not just likes)
- **NO strong monetization** (no link in bio selling anything, no pinned product posts)
- **Engagement rate >3%** (they're active, not dead)

**Why:** These convert fastest. They have the attention. They just don't have the system.

### 💬 DM — Replace Soft Openers With This

**HIGH INTENT OPENER (use this one):**
```
You've already done the hard part — you built attention.

Quick question… are you actually getting paid from it yet?
```

**STACK WITH THE AUDIT HOOK:**
```
I just audited a page with your follower count.
They were leaving $3K–$8K/month on the table.
Want me to show you what I found?
```

### ⚔️ Call Close Framework

On every discovery call:

**Step 1 — Diagnose:**
> "Walk me through how you're making money right now."

**Step 2 — Expose the gap:**
> "So you've got [X]K followers… solid engagement… but no system converting that into revenue."

**Step 3 — Future pace:**
> "If nothing changes, where are you in 3 months? Same place. Right."

**Step 4 — Solution:**
> "We install the system behind what you already built. Attention → revenue."

**Step 5 — Close:**
> "We can start with Quick Flip ($990, 48 hours, you walk away with a live offer) — or go Full Engine if you want the whole machine built. What makes more sense?"

**Rule:** Don't pitch. Diagnose → reveal → close.
