# Client Onboarding Checklist — Ghost Engine

## Quick Flip ($500-2K | 48hr delivery)
- [ ] Collect platform credentials (IG, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Get Stripe/PayPal access or create new
- [ ] Content audit — review last 30 posts, top performers
- [ ] Offer creation — core offer, price, guarantee
- [ ] Landing page build (Carrd/Systeme.io)
- [ ] Stripe payment link configured
- [ ] Bio link updated with CTA
- [ ] 3 content templates (hook, story, CTA)
- [ ] DM scripts for warm leads
- [ ] Delivery <48hrs, handoff call 30min

## Full Engine ($2-5K/mo | 2-week onboarding)
*Everything in Quick Flip PLUS:*
- [ ] Funnel: opt-in, tripwire, core offer, upsell
- [ ] Email sequences: welcome(5), abandon(3), nurture(weekly)
- [ ] GetResponse automations configured
- [ ] Content calendar — 30 days, 4 pillars
- [ ] Ad creatives — 3 static, 2 video scripts
- [ ] Calendly booking page
- [ ] Lead magnet (PDF/video/quiz)
- [ ] Weekly reporting dashboard
- [ ] Onboarding 60min + Week 2 check-in 30min

## Ghost Partner (Rev share 20-30% | ongoing)
*Everything in Full Engine PLUS:*
- [ ] Revenue share agreement signed
- [ ] Joint Stripe or split payment setup
- [ ] Monthly strategy call 60min (1st Monday)
- [ ] Quarterly business review 90min
- [ ] KPIs: revenue, leads, conversion, CAC
- [ ] Full ghost content management
- [ ] Ad spend management + optimization
- [ ] Monthly P&L + revenue reconciliation
- [ ] 90-day exit clause documented

## Universal (all tiers)
- [ ] Welcome email with onboarding doc
- [ ] Slack/Discord channel created
- [ ] Added to CRM (GetResponse + Notion)
- [ ] Kickoff call recorded and shared
- [ ] Timeline + milestones confirmed
